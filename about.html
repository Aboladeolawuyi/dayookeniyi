About Dayo Okeniyi & Co.

A trusted firm of Estate Surveyors, Valuers and Real Estate Advisors, committed to integrity, professionalism and value delivery.

Our Firm

Dayo Okeniyi & Co. is a professional firm of Estate Surveyors, Valuers and Facilities Managers duly registered under the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA) of Nigeria. The firm is a corporate member of the Nigerian Institution of Estate Surveyors and Valuers (NIESV) and is fully regulated by the Estate Surveyors and Valuers Registration Board of Nigeria (ESVARBON).
Our sister company SHELTER NEST REALTY LIMITED is a firm of real estate consultant speacilzes in real estate Agency and Marketing.

We provide comprehensive real estate solutions to individuals, corporate organizations, financial institutions and government agencies across Nigeria. Our services are designed to protect client interests, optimize asset performance and deliver measurable value in an increasingly dynamic property market.

Our Vision

To be a leading and trusted real estate advisory and services firm in Nigeria, recognized for professional excellence, integrity, innovation and consistent delivery of superior value.

To deliver comprehensive, ethical and client-focused real estate solutions through expert valuation, agency, advisory, project management and property & facility management services — guided by professionalism, transparency and excellence.

Managing Partner

ESV (Dr.) Oladayo Femi Okeniyi

MNIVS, RSV, PFD, PhD

ESV (Dr.) Oladayo Femi Okeniyi is the Managing Partner of Dayo Okeniyi & Co. He holds a Bachelor of Technology degree in Estate Management and Valuation and is a registered Estate Surveyor and Valuer in Nigeria.

With over twelve (12) years of uninterrupted professional private practice, he has successfully executed valuation assignments, estate agency transactions, property and facility management briefs, project management and feasibility studies across diverse sectors of the real estate market.

His professional practice is defined by strong technical competence, strategic thinking and a commitment to ethical service delivery.
