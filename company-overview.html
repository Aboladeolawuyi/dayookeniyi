Company Overview

A trusted firm of Estate Surveyors, Valuers and Real Estate Advisors, committed to integrity, professionalism and value delivery.

Our Firm

DAYO OKENIYI & CO., is a professional firm of Estate Surveyors, Valuers and Facilities Management duly registered with Corporate Affairs Commission under the Companies and Allied Matters Act of Nigeria (CAMA), a corporate member of the Nigerian Institution of Estate Surveyors and Valuers (NIESV), as well as the Estate Surveyors and Valuers Registration Board of Nigeria (ESVARBON) to practice and provide real estate services in nationwide. Our sister company, SHELTER NEST REALTY LLC, is a premier real estate firm specializing in innovative property development, real estate agency services, and high-impact market positioning.

With a commitment to integrity, professionalism, and customer satisfaction, we provide comprehensive solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of organizations, buyers, sellers, landlords, and tenants. Our aim is to exceed client expectations and establish long-term relationships built on trust.

Over the years, our team has consistently achieved exceptional results, exceeding targets and setting new benchmarks in the real estate market. This has earned us industry accolades and awards, acknowledging our commitment to excellence, professionalism, and customer satisfaction. Our commitment to building lasting relationships and delivering results-driven service has resulted in a strong network of satisfied clients who continue to entrust us with their real estate needs.

We provide comprehensive real estate solutions to individuals, corporate organizations, financial institutions and government agencies across Nigeria. Our services are designed to protect client interests, optimize asset performance and deliver measurable value in an increasingly dynamic property market.
